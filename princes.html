Design Challenge

Princes Theatre

UI/UX Designer

Prince’s theatre is a small independent cinema chain in suburban Melbourne. Until now the theatre’s owners have relied on non-digital forms of advertising to maintain their business. The cinema has always focused on a mature audience. Recently they’ve noticed a younger demographic with different interests and preferences overtaking them as their largest segment. This unintentional generational shift has meant that the cinema is not poised to cater for their needs. They’re lagging a fair way behind their competitions digital experience due to under-investment. Their website is old and lacks the features of their competitors
